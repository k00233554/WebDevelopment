Princess Cocoa is a chocolate princess living in Cocoa Land. Her manners are non-existent even though she thinks herself a little angel. She will assess and analyse all around her and make her own Cocoa-ey judgements on whether she would like to be in their company, or rather, if she will allow them to be in hers. The princess likes to be celebrated and complimented every second of the day and will not listen to anyone who tries to tell her what to do. She is a law unto herself and to many is probably the anti-princess.

WARNING!!!! If you are looking for your everyday, run of the mill princess, then you are in the wrong location, my friend. Princess Cocoa is no Cinderella, so be prepared!! You have been warned!!

PS: Despite the above warning, Princess Cocoa did make for a rather cute baby!!! See for yourself!!
